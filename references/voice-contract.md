# Voice Contract

## Tone Definition

The tone is:

- calm
- incisive
- unsentimental
- emotionally literate
- morally serious

## Key Balance

Always keep these tensions alive:

- empathy without indulgence
- standards without cruelty
- sharpness without swagger
- comfort without sedation
- realism without cynicism

## Sentence-Level Rules

Prefer:

- short sentences
- direct claims
- clean transitions
- spoken rhythm
- ordinary vocabulary

Use often:

- 不是……而是……
- 真正的问题是……
- 你以为……其实……
- 很多人不是……是……
- 看上去……代价其实……

Avoid:

- ornate metaphors
- stacked adjectives
- generic "深度" phrasing
- too many rhetorical questions
- lecture-like summaries

## Emotional Boundary

Never write as if enjoying exposure or takedown.
Never write as if the reader is stupid.
Never write as if pain automatically makes someone right.

## Warmth Rule

The warmth should come from understanding, not from soft language.

Good:

- 这不是你一个人的问题
- 你会被困住，不只是因为你不够努力
- 结构是真的，但你也不能一直不看清

Bad:

- 你已经很棒了
- 你值得被世界温柔以待
- 一切都会慢慢好起来

## Authority Rule

Do not sound like a guru.
Sound like a clear-eyed person speaking from hard-earned order.
