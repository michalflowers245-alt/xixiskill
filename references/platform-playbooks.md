# Platform Playbooks

This file turns the core voice into repeatable output patterns by format.
Use it when the task is not just "write like Xixi" but "write like Xixi for a specific format."

## Short Video 3-5 Minutes

Best for:

- education topics
- work and money traps
- family and boundary topics
- opinion topics that need structure without becoming lectures

### Default Structure

1. Hard opening
2. Rewrite the question
3. Explain why most people talk about it too shallowly
4. Use one concrete story, scene, or case as material spine
5. Give 3-4 structured points
6. Return to the ordinary person's predicament
7. Close with a line that restores judgment

### Recommended Opening Types

- 很多人一聊这个问题，开口就是两句废话。
- 先说结论，这件事真正的问题不是 X，是 Y。
- 这个题最容易讲错的地方，是大家都在回答一个太浅的问题。

### Source Rule

- use Lao Zhou logic to rewrite the question
- use XiaoLin logic to choose the case, story, or explanatory sequence
- use Xixi voice to land the emotional and moral pressure

### Recommended Rhythm

- short paragraphs
- sentence clusters of 1-3 lines
- obvious pauses after judgment lines
- one clear structural turn every 20-30 seconds

### What To Avoid

- too much data dumping
- too much rhetorical performance
- too many metaphors
- ending with emotional softness

## Xiaohongshu / Image Post

Best for:

- advice traps
- boundary insights
- workplace and relationship cognition
- 3-point or 4-point breakdowns

### Default Structure

1. Strong title
2. One sharp opening sentence
3. 3-5 point breakdown
4. One Xixi-style closing line

### Good Title Types

- 这 3 句听起来很对的话，正在慢慢毁掉普通人
- 很多人不是不会努力，是一直在用错力
- 真正让人越活越差的，不是不努力，是判断外包

### Writing Rule

Keep each point visually short.
Do not over-explain.
Each slide or section should deliver one clean cut.

## Long Article

Best for:

- college / education pieces
- growth and self-deception
- family and subjectivity
- broader themes that need more than one scene

### Default Structure

1. Opening judgment
2. What people usually get wrong
3. The real question
4. Structural explanation
5. Why ordinary people still get trapped
6. Practical cognitive conclusion

### Long-Form Advantage

Use long form when:

- the topic needs multiple layers
- the cost distribution matters
- the reader must feel a progression from confusion to order

Do not use long form just to sound serious.

## Topic Diagnosis

Best for:

- deciding whether a topic fits Xixi
- ranking topics
- sharpening weak angles

### Output Template

```md
# 先说结论

{这个题值不值得做}

# 这个题真正该讲的，不是表面那层

{重写问题}

# 为什么它适合你 / 为什么不适合你

- {优势}
- {风险}

# 更适合你的改法

{新的 framing}

# 最后给一个能开拍的标题

{标题}
```

## Rewrite / Ghost Editing

Best for:

- user-provided drafts
- cleaning AI smell
- making a text feel more like Xixi

### Required Sequence

1. Diagnose the original
2. Name what is missing
3. Rewrite in Xixi's voice

### Common Missing Things

- no rewritten question
- no structure
- no ordinary person perspective
- too much softness or too much cheap sharpness
- weak ending
