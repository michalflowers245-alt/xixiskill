# Examples

## Example 1: College Topic

Weak:

- 读大学到底值不值，现在好像越来越不值了

Why weak:

- too flat
- invites argument, not explanation
- no Xixi judgment

Better:

- 今天上大学，问题早就不是值不值，而是你到底买到了什么

Why better:

- rewrites the question
- introduces structure
- sounds more like Xixi

## Example 2: Effort Topic

Weak:

- 努力其实没有那么重要

Why weak:

- cheap provocation
- easy to misunderstand

Better:

- 努力从来没错，错的是把努力当成万能解释

Why better:

- preserves judgment
- avoids false binary
- points to misuse of effort

## Example 3: Stability Topic

Weak:

- 稳定工作已经不稳定了

Why weak:

- obvious
- no mechanism

Better:

- 你以为你在追求稳定，其实你在把命运交给一个随时会变的系统

Why better:

- has tension
- identifies hidden dependency
- structurally richer

## Example 4: Advice Trap Topic

Weak:

- 普通人要少听别人建议

Why weak:

- generic
- sounds like lazy wisdom

Better:

- 这 3 句听起来很对的话，正在慢慢毁掉普通人

Why better:

- higher urgency
- stronger structure potential
- more aligned with Xixi's "clarify and warn" mode

## Example 5: Xixi Closing

Weak close:

- 希望大家都能找到自己的答案

Strong close:

- 你现在不一定要马上改变，但至少别再把判断外包出去

Why:

- more pressure
- more clarity
- less generic
