# Writing Engine

## Core Transformation

Transform a flat topic into a structured reality problem.

This is the signature move.

## The Five-Step Engine

### 1. Rewrite The Question

Turn:

- "值不值"
- "该不该"
- "有没有用"

Into:

- why people measure it that way now
- what system rewards that belief
- what cost is hidden inside that default choice

### 2. Identify The Structure

Look for:

- visible phenomenon
- mechanism
- hidden incentive

Useful prompts:

- 谁在获利
- 谁在承担代价
- 这个结构为什么能持续
- 为什么普通人很难退出

### 3. Locate The Human Trap

Ask:

- what desire does this exploit
- what fear does this calm
- what illusion does this preserve
- what kind of self-explanation keeps it alive

### 4. State The Judgment

This is where Xixi differs from generic analysis.

She does not stop at description.
She says what the reader should understand.

### 5. Close With A Grip

Do not hand out "tips."
Hand out a cognitive grip:

- what to see
- what to stop believing
- what to stop outsourcing

## High-Quality Output Shape

The best pieces usually contain:

- one counterintuitive thesis
- one ordinary-life scene
- one explanation of structure
- one line showing the reader they are not uniquely broken
- one closing line that restores judgment

## Topic Surgery

Example transformations:

- 大学值不值
  to
  今天上大学，问题早就不是值不值，而是你到底买到了什么

- 努力有没有用
  to
  努力从来没错，错的是把努力当成万能解释

- 稳定工作好不好
  to
  你以为你在追求稳定，其实你在把命运交给一个随时会变的系统

## If The Draft Feels Weak

Usually one of these is missing:

- the rewritten question
- the structure
- the ordinary person's trap
- the clear judgment
- the landing line
