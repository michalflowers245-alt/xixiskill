# Transformations

This file shows how to move from generic copy to Xixi-style copy.
The goal is not decoration.
The goal is a change in thinking pressure.

## Transformation 1: From Opinion To Structure

Generic:

- 大学到底值不值

Xixi:

- 今天上大学，问题早就不是值不值，而是你到底买到了什么

What changed:

- replaces argument bait with a deeper question
- creates room for structure, class, return, and expectation

## Transformation 2: From Provocation To Serious Judgment

Generic:

- 努力其实没那么重要

Xixi:

- 努力从来没错，错的是把努力当成万能解释

What changed:

- removes cheap provocation
- keeps conflict but adds precision

## Transformation 3: From Obvious Statement To Hidden Dependency

Generic:

- 稳定工作也不稳定了

Xixi:

- 你以为你在追求稳定，其实你在把命运交给一个随时会变的系统

What changed:

- shifts from bland observation to structural dependence
- makes the reader feel the hidden cost

## Transformation 4: From Advice To Warning

Generic:

- 不要总听别人的建议

Xixi:

- 这 3 句听起来很对的话，正在慢慢毁掉普通人

What changed:

- higher stakes
- more urgency
- clearer fit with Xixi's warning-and-explain mode

## Transformation 5: From Healing To Clarity

Generic:

- 希望你能慢慢找到真正的自己

Xixi:

- 你现在不一定要马上改变，但至少别再把判断外包出去

What changed:

- less softness
- more cognitive pressure
- stronger ending identity

## Transformation Rules

When rewriting into Xixi's voice, aim for these shifts:

1. Replace empty abstraction with a visible scene or mechanism.
2. Replace generic encouragement with a judgment plus a grip.
3. Replace shallow opposition with a rewritten question.
4. Replace emotional heat with structural clarity.
5. Replace performance depth with clean pressure.
