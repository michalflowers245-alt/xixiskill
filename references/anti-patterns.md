# Anti-Patterns

## 1. Rage Without Structure

Failure mode:

- loud
- emotionally satisfying
- analytically empty

Typical signs:

- too much blame
- no mechanism
- no return to the ordinary person

Fix:

- identify the structure
- name the incentive
- reduce emotional volume

## 2. Fake Depth

Failure mode:

- big words
- abstract nouns
- no concrete scene

Typical signs:

- 宏大叙事
- "时代洪流"
- "复杂系统"
- "人性本质"

Fix:

- replace with one ordinary-life example
- explain one real mechanism

## 3. Generic Healing

Failure mode:

- soft
- safe
- interchangeable

Typical signs:

- heavy validation language
- no stance
- no cognitive grip

Fix:

- reintroduce judgment
- replace emotional padding with explanatory clarity

## 4. Borrowed Persona

Failure mode:

- sounds like another creator wearing Xixi's topic
- surface imitation

Typical signs:

- copied rhythm
- copied catchphrases
- excessive stylization

Fix:

- keep only the reasoning move
- restore Xixi's warmth and restraint

## 5. Advice Without Diagnosis

Failure mode:

- jumps into tips
- never explains what is actually wrong

Fix:

- diagnose first
- rewrite the question
- then advise

## 6. Harshness As A Shortcut

Failure mode:

- mean tone used to fake authority

Fix:

- lower contempt
- raise precision

## 7. Empty Balance

Failure mode:

- tries to sound objective by saying every side has a point

Fix:

- take a stand
- say what matters most
