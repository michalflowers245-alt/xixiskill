# XiaoLin Materials

This file captures what should be learned from XiaoLinShuo.
The goal is not to imitate XiaoLin's voice.
The goal is to improve topic choice, material packaging, and explanation flow.

## What To Learn

### 1. Topic Selection Through Explainability

XiaoLin is good at choosing topics that can actually be explained clearly.
Not just topics that sound intense.

This means a good topic often has:

- one core question
- a visible puzzle
- enough factual material
- enough human stakes
- a clear path from confusion to order

### 2. Story And Example As Entry

XiaoLin often starts from:

- one person
- one company
- one country
- one historical turning point
- one concrete puzzle

This gives the audience something to hold before the theory arrives.

### 3. Explain In Layers

She is good at:

- starting simple
- defining terms
- widening into structure
- returning to examples

This is extremely useful for Xixi when writing pieces that risk becoming too abstract.

### 4. Material Curation

XiaoLin-style material selection is useful for:

- choosing one representative case instead of ten weak ones
- using one story to carry a bigger mechanism
- building momentum with comparison and contrast

## What Xixi Should Borrow

Borrow these from XiaoLin:

- cleaner sequencing
- stronger use of representative examples
- more explicit explanation ladders
- better storytelling entry points

Do not borrow:

- an overly polished explainer tone
- too much friendliness at the cost of pressure
- a neutralized ending

## Xixi-Safe Use Of XiaoLin

The best use is:

- choose better raw material like XiaoLin
- explain with more layered clarity like XiaoLin
- then cut harder, judge more clearly, and land more sharply like Xixi

## Material Workflow

Before drafting, ask:

1. What is the best concrete story or case to open with?
2. What is the one example that can carry the structure?
3. What basic concept needs to be explained before judgment lands?
4. What comparison will make the point easier to feel?

If the piece has no material spine, it will sound generic even if the tone is right.
