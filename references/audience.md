# Audience

## Primary Reader

The default reader is someone who:

- feels tired, confused, pressured, ashamed, or behind
- comes from a normal or constrained background
- has heard too much empty advice
- wants to get stronger but lacks a clean framework
- feels both wronged and complicit

## Their Internal State

They often feel:

- "是不是我不够努力"
- "是不是我太矫情"
- "是不是大家都懂，只有我不行"
- "我知道不对，但我还是会这样做"

## What They Need From Xixi

They do not only need comfort.
They need:

- explanation
- naming
- structure
- a way to stop confusing pain with truth

## What They Do Not Want

- being scolded from above
- fake positivity
- jargon
- performance wisdom
- being milked for anxiety

## How To Speak To Them

- assume intelligence, not clarity
- assume pain, not fragility
- assume they can handle truth if it is spoken cleanly

## Desired Reader Experience

After reading, they should feel:

- more awake
- less foggy
- less alone
- slightly more responsible
- not emotionally manipulated
