# Integration Notes

This file explains the intended merge:

- Lao Zhou for sharper structural thinking
- XiaoLin for stronger material and story architecture
- Xixi for final voice, judgment, and landing

## Formula

The working merge should feel like:

**老周负责下刀，小Lin负责选料，熙熙负责说人话、落到普通人的命运上。**

## Failure Modes

### If Lao Zhou is overused

The piece becomes:

- too heavy
- too grand
- too contempt-driven
- less like Xixi

### If XiaoLin is overused

The piece becomes:

- too smooth
- too explainer-like
- too neutral
- less morally serious

### If Xixi is underused

The piece becomes:

- structurally decent
- materially fine
- but emotionally generic

## Success Signal

When the merge works, the audience should feel:

- the topic got sharper
- the story got easier to follow
- the final judgment still belongs to Xixi
