# Territory

## Primary Content Lanes

### 1. Education, College, Class, Return On Effort

Strong because:

- Xixi has a natural speaking position here
- these topics connect reality, family expectation, class, and self-worth
- they allow structural explanation without heavy technical barriers

Representative angles:

- 读大学到底买到了什么
- 努力为什么越来越难自动兑现
- 好学生为什么更容易输给现实

### 2. Family, Boundaries, Emotional Roots

Strong because:

- there is genuine emotional truth here
- the voice can combine pain, clarity, and standards
- this is where empathy and structure can coexist well

Representative angles:

- 为什么很多人长大后还是不敢替自己做决定
- “为你好”为什么会毁掉主体性
- 安全感为什么有时会把人养废

### 3. Work, Money, Risk, Decision Traps

Strong because:

- highly relatable
- easier to generate traction
- compatible with structural explanation and practical cognition

Representative angles:

- 稳定工作为什么越来越像幻觉
- 普通人最容易被收割的几种“好建议”
- 很多人穷，不是赚得少，是决策太贵

## Secondary Lane

### AI And New Things, But In Ordinary Language

Only do this if:

- the explanation is simple
- the angle serves ordinary people
- the content stays grounded in real life, not novelty worship

## Weak Or Risky Territory

### Pure Finance

Risk:

- can sound under-supported
- may create false authority

Only enter with strong evidence and narrow claims.

### Macro Social Commentary

Risk:

- easy to become large and empty
- easy to slide into posture

### Gender Conflict And Sexual Politics

Risk:

- attracts the wrong audience
- raises noise faster than trust
- can dilute Xixi's core positioning

### Pure Healing Content

Risk:

- removes judgment
- weakens identity
- makes the voice look interchangeable

## Topic Filter

Before taking a topic, ask:

1. Is this about ordinary people's real predicament?
2. Can Xixi say something structurally true here?
3. Can this be explained without pretending expertise?
4. Will this deepen the account's人格密度 instead of scattering it?

If fewer than 3 answers are yes, do not prioritize the topic.
