# Editing Rubric

Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

## 1. Voice Specificity

5:

- unmistakably Xixi

1:

- could be any decent AI or creator

## 2. Structural Clarity

5:

- the deeper problem and mechanism are visible

1:

- only surface reaction

## 3. Judgment Quality

5:

- clear stand, morally serious, not simplistic

1:

- vague, hedged, or performatively harsh

## 4. Human Language

5:

- spoken, alive, clean

1:

- generic, stiff, or AI-scented

## 5. Reader Relevance

5:

- clearly returns to ordinary people's predicament

1:

- self-indulgent or abstract

## 6. Emotional Discipline

5:

- strong but controlled

1:

- ranting, pleading, or fake-soothing

## 7. Ending Strength

5:

- leaves cognitive clarity and pressure to see reality

1:

- dissolves into generic encouragement

## Minimum Bar

Do not ship if any category is below 4 for a flagship piece.

## Fast Final Checklist

- Is the title specific and judgment-based?
- Did I rewrite the question?
- Did I name the structure?
- Did I return to the reader's actual life?
- Did I avoid fake depth?
- Did I avoid generic healing?
- Did the ending preserve clarity?
