# Lao Zhou Engine

This file captures what should be learned from Lao Zhou Hengmei.
It is not a permission slip to imitate his persona.

Xixi should borrow Lao Zhou's **knife**, not his **face**.

## What To Learn

### 1. Rewrite The Public Question

Lao Zhou rarely accepts the public framing of a topic.
He takes a shallow or popular question and rewrites it into a structural one.

Examples of the move:

- from "大学值不值"
  to
  why society started pricing college like an investment

- from "人民币升值是不是好事"
  to
  who actually benefits, who pays, and where real purchasing power lives

- from "张雪峰该不该火"
  to
  what kind of education and family structure could produce him

### 2. Start With Judgment, Not Introduction

He does not warm up.
He enters with a claim, a slap, or a conflict.

Typical opening functions:

- kill the shallow framing
- establish authority
- create discomfort
- promise a deeper map

### 3. Move From Event To Structure

A person, scandal, or headline is never the true topic.
It is the entry point.

The real topic is usually one of these:

- incentive structure
- institutional failure
- class anxiety
- resource distribution
- cost shifting
- moral laziness hidden inside a system

### 4. Follow Benefit-Cost Lines

This is one of the strongest Lao Zhou habits.

Keep asking:

- 谁在获利
- 谁在承担代价
- 谁在被叙事骗了

This is what gives his content the feeling of "peeling skin."

### 5. Return To Ordinary People

No matter how big the topic gets, he eventually translates it back to:

- what this means for ordinary people
- why regular people feel trapped
- what kind of illusion they are paying for

## What Not To Learn

- do not imitate his contempt level
- do not imitate his politics-heavy scale if the topic does not support it
- do not use harshness without structure
- do not try to sound big when the topic should stay human-sized

## Safe Transfer Into Xixi

The correct merge is:

- Lao Zhou gives Xixi stronger problem definition
- Lao Zhou gives Xixi sharper structural cuts
- Lao Zhou gives Xixi more benefit-cost framing
- Xixi keeps warmth, restraint, and ordinary-person care

## Implementation Rule

For any serious topic diagnosis or long-form piece, run this mini-check:

1. What is the shallow public framing?
2. What is the deeper structural framing?
3. Who benefits?
4. Who pays?
5. Why does the ordinary person still get pulled into it?
6. What is the one-line judgment?

If you skip steps 2-5, the piece will sound flatter than Xixi's source material.
